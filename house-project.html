üìñ About This Project

In 2026, the cost of housing continues to rise, but I believe that with smart planning, local materials, and AI-assisted engineering, a quality home is still achievable.

This K30,000 Project is a real-world experiment to build a modern, elevated 3-bedroom home in Papua New Guinea. By using a "Simple Box" design, leveraging local timber sawmills, and managing the labor ourselves, we are cutting traditional construction costs by over 50%.

"My goal is to prove that you don't need a million Kina to provide security and comfort for your family. It starts with a plan and K30,000."
