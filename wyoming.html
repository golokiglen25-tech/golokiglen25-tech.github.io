Wyoming LLC Scaling Roadmap

Turning YouTube Views into a Protected Business Asset

1

Name Your Wyoming LLC

Ensure your business name is unique and ends with "LLC" or "L.L.C." Check availability on the Wyoming SOS website.

2

Appoint a Registered Agent

Crucial for privacy. Use a registered agent service in Wyoming so your personal home address stays off public records.

3

File Articles of Organization

This is the document that makes it official. The filing fee is $100 ($102 online). This is your "Business Birth Certificate."

4

Get Your EIN from the IRS

Obtain your Employer Identification Number. This allows you to open a business bank account and fund your Social Ads Manager.

5

Draft an Operating Agreement

Establish how your LLC is managed. This is vital for maintaining the "Corporate Veil" and protecting your assets.
