Meet the Founder

Glen Goloki

Welcome to TGL Wealth Hub. I am a professional Electrical Tradesman based in Papua New Guinea with a deep commitment to electrical safety and code compliance.

Having spent years on the tools, I understand the practical challenges tradespeople face every day. My goal is to master the National Electrical Code (NEC) and share that knowledge with the global community while building modern digital income streams.

The TGL Mission

I founded TGL Wealth Hub to help fellow tradespeople transition from just "working a job" to building a professional brand. We focus on:

  • Field-Tested Knowledge: Practical breakdowns of the 2026 NEC (Articles 120 and 130) from a tradesman's perspective.
  • Digital Growth: Showing professionals how to use AI and digital marketing to scale their income beyond the hourly rate.
  • Compliance Tools: High-quality checklists and guides designed for use on actual job sites.

Real-World Experience

From residential wiring to commercial floodlight installations and daylight switch systems, I bring real-world experience to every guide I write. TGL Wealth Hub is where the grit of the electrical trade meets the innovation of the digital world.
