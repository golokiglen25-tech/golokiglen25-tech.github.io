Clause 8.2.2 requirements (De-energized conditions)

Clause 8.3 Testing Protocols

Tests must be conducted strictly in this sequential order

  1. 1
    Earthing System Continuity Main earth electrode resistance must be ≤0.5Ω.
  2. 2
    Insulation Resistance Min 500V DC test. Insulation resistance must be ≥1.0 MΩ.
  3. 3
    Polarity Testing Ensure active wires connect to active terminals, neutrals to neutrals, etc.
  4. 4
    Correct Circuit Connection Verify active/neutral connections belong to the same protective loop.
  5. 5
    Earth Fault Loop Impedance (EFLI) LIVE check. Zs must be lower than calculated maximum threshold (Zmax).
  6. 6
    RCD Trip Time Operation Safety switches must disconnect within 300 ms at 30mA residual leakage.
