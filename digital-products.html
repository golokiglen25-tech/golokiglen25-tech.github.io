Digital Product Mastery

Building a sustainable online business doesn't require high-ticket offers. In 2026, the "Micro-Product" market is booming.

The $5 - $10 Strategy

By pricing products between $5 and $10, you lower the "barrier to entry" for customers. This allows for higher volume sales and easier customer acquisition through platforms like TikTok and Instagram.

Why it works: At this price point, most customers view the purchase as a "no-brainer" investment in their own productivity or education.

Top Products for 2026

  • Electrical Engineering Checklists: Step-by-step guides for common residential inspections.
  • AI Prompt Libraries: Niche-specific prompts for small business owners.
  • Budgeting Templates: Simple, clean Google Sheets for personal finance.
